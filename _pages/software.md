## R Packages

**rcttext**: Tools for planning and analyzing randomized experiments with text-based outcomes. Includes functions for model-assisted impact estimation, power analysis, and sample size calculations for text-based randomized controlled trials.<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <img src="../images/github-mark.png" width="20" height="15"> [[GitHub]](https://github.com/reaganmozer/rcttext)

**stratsampling**: Stratified sampling methods for model-assisted estimation with surrogate outcomes. Implements the methods described in Mozer, Pashley, and Miratrix (2026).<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <img src="../images/github-mark.png" width="20" height="15"> [[GitHub]](https://github.com/lmiratrix/stratsampling)

**textmatch**: Tools for matching text documents and performing quality and balance assessments in studies where covariates and/or outcomes are defined by text. Implements the methods described in Mozer, Miratrix, Kaufman, and Anastasopoulos (2020).<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <img src="../images/github-mark.png" width="20" height="15"> [[GitHub]](https://github.com/reaganmozer/textmatch) &nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; [![DOI](https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.2626730.svg)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.2626730)
